About Inqubeko

Inqubeko Consulting Engineers is a Consulting Engineering practice established in Zululand (Northern Kwa-Zulu Natal) region in 2005, concentrating on civil and structural engineering as well as project management.  The company is managed from offices situated at 5 Paul Avenue, Empangeni.

The company was established by Mr. Freek Pretorius (Pr. Eng.), a former director of VGC Consulting Engineers in Richards Bay. In 2007 Mr Jaco Claase (Pr Tech Eng) joined the company as a director concentrating on Structural and Civil Engineering projects.

The company is managed from offices situated at 5 Paul Avenue, Empangeni.
